Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 17, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Discord Quiz Activity ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our Discord Activity application.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following information:

  • Discord User Information: Username, user ID, and avatar (provided by Discord SDK)
  • Game Data: Quiz scores, answer selections, and response times during gameplay
  • Session Data: Room IDs and player participation data (temporary, session-only)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the quiz game functionality
  • Track scores and gameplay progress during sessions
  • Enable multiplayer features and real-time synchronization
  • Display player names and scores to other participants in the same game

4. Data Storage and Retention

All game data is stored temporarily in memory during active gameplay sessions. We do not permanently store quiz answers, scores, or gameplay history. Session data is automatically cleared when:

  • Players leave the activity
  • The game session ends
  • The server restarts

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personal information to third parties. Your Discord information is only used within the game session and is visible only to other players in the same game room.

6. Discord Integration

This application is a Discord Activity and operates within Discord's ecosystem. By using this activity, you are also subject to Discord's Privacy Policy. We access Discord user information only through Discord's official Embedded App SDK.

7. Children's Privacy

Our service is intended for users who meet Discord's minimum age requirements. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13 (or the applicable age in your jurisdiction).

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Stop using the activity at any time
  • Request information about data we process during your session
  • Know that no permanent records of your gameplay are stored

9. Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information during gameplay sessions. All communication between clients and servers uses secure protocols.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last Updated" date at the top indicates when the policy was last revised.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through Discord or via the project repository.
